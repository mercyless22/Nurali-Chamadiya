GD (Group Discussion) Activities

Group discussion (GD) is an effective learning and assessment tool, particularly in leadership, academic, and recruiting contexts. Group discussion is a mass elimination tool during the selection process. GDs simulate real-life team interactions where people must listen, think, and speak effectively in a group, and GDs also encourage participants to consider diverse perspectives, refine their own thinking, and arrive at a balanced viewpoint. In addition, the GDs also check skills like listening, comprehension, critical reasoning, teamwork and collaboration, leadership, and time management.
