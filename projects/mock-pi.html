Industry Led Mock Interviews

At Marwadi University, the interested students undergo the mock interview. The objective is to empower the students to confidently navigate personal interview rounds and subsequently secure coveted placements in high-paying positions. During the mock personal interview, the participants face a one-on-one and panel interview. These interviews pose a mix of technical and non-technical questions directly related to skills highlighted in the resume that can give the student a fair understanding of interview skills and valuable insights on areas of improvement.
